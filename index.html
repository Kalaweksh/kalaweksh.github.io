I'm Jonathan, an aspiring data analyst/scientist. I am excited to share some of my projects with you and hope they will give you a good understanding of my capabilities and potential.

These projects are meant to showcase my ability to work with data, draw insights, and communicate findings effectively.

Work

ScholarViz

ScholarViz is a light application built on Semantic Scholar that takes an input query in the form of a research topic and returns a visual chart of papers in that field.

FootyPredict

FootyPredict is a Jupyter notebook using data from football-data.co.uk to build machine learning models to predict the future performance of football teams.
